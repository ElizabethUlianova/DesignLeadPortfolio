Sbermarket

Senior Product Designer

Designing a team communication platform for 10,000 employees.

Main tasks ✔

Internal communication platform

Designing a unified communication and collaboration tool for a 10,000-person company — replacing a fragmented mix of messengers, email, and wiki tools.

Complex permission model

Designing a flexible but understandable permissions system that could handle the complexity of a large organisation with multiple departments, teams, and roles.

Adoption strategy

Working with HR and internal comms on a design-led adoption strategy — making onboarding so smooth that employees defaulted to the new tool without needing mandates.

Key results 🎁

85% adoption in 3 months

Voluntary adoption reached 85% within 3 months of launch — above the target of 70% and ahead of schedule.

Reduced email volume

Internal email volume dropped by 35% in the first quarter, with teams reporting faster decisions and less time in unproductive meetings.

High NPS

Internal NPS of 52 after 3 months — unusually high for an enterprise internal tool. Key driver: the product felt like a consumer app, not enterprise software.

Process

The biggest challenge was designing for a wide range of users in a single product — from warehouse workers checking shift schedules to executives running strategic planning. The information architecture had to be simple enough for the former without feeling limiting to the latter.

I ran user research across 6 departments, built personas representing the key user types, and used those to make prioritisation decisions throughout the project. The most important insight: most employees only needed 3–4 features reliably — so we made those excellent before expanding scope.
