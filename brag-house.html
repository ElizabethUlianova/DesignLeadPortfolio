Brag House

Senior Product Designer

Designing the MVP of the esports app that raised a total of $6.7M in investment.

Main tasks ✔

MVP design for investment

Designing a compelling MVP that could demonstrate the product vision clearly to investors — balancing ambition with what was actually buildable in the timeline.

Esports community product

Designing social and competitive features for a highly engaged, opinionated audience — tournament brackets, team profiles, bragging mechanics, and live match integration.

Brand and product alignment

Working closely with the founders to ensure the visual identity and product experience felt authentic to the esports community and differentiated in a crowded market.

Key results 🎁

$6.7M raised

The product design was a key part of the investor pitch deck and demo. The app's visual quality and clarity of concept contributed directly to closing the funding round.

Strong community adoption

10,000+ users in the first 3 months post-launch, with strong organic word-of-mouth in the gaming community due to the social features.

Shipped fast

Full MVP designed and shipped in 8 weeks — rapid iteration with daily standups and tight feedback loops with the founding team.

Process

The project moved at startup speed — rapid discovery, daily design reviews, and an iterative approach that prioritised learning over perfection. The key early question was: what makes esports fans want to "brag"? — and the answer shaped the core social mechanics of the product.

I ran competitive benchmarking across gaming apps and social platforms, identified the unique angle Brag House could own, and translated that into a design language that felt native to the gaming world while remaining accessible to mainstream users.
