Senior Product Designer

Designing a new streaming service from scratch — from zero to launch.

Main tasks ✔

Building from zero

Designed the full streaming product from scratch — app structure, navigation patterns, content discovery, playback experience, and subscription flows.

Multi-platform design

Designed for mobile (iOS & Android), web, Smart TV, and tablet — adapting the experience for each platform's constraints while maintaining a unified visual language.

Design system creation

Built the design system from the ground up — tokens, components, documentation — making it ready for a team to scale with.

Key results 🎁

Shipped on schedule

Launched the MVP on time across 4 platforms. The tight timeline required clear prioritisation and frequent alignment with engineering.

Strong user retention

D7 retention above industry average for new streaming services. The onboarding and content discovery flow were credited as key drivers.

Scalable foundation

The design system enabled 2× faster feature delivery in the 6 months post-launch, with consistent quality across the expanding team.

Process

The project started with extensive competitive analysis and user research — understanding what viewers valued in existing streaming services and where the gaps were. This informed the core IA decisions that shaped the whole product.

I worked in close collaboration with the product manager and engineering lead, running weekly design reviews and using rapid prototyping to test navigation hypotheses before committing to builds.

The biggest challenge was designing a compelling content discovery experience without a large library at launch — the solution was a curated, editorial-first approach that made a small catalogue feel rich and intentional.
