My name is Lisa and I'm a Product Design Lead and a strong Individual Contributor with 6+ years of experience in B2C and e-commerce.

My approach

People Management 👥

I grow people on purpose

I notice small progress early and reinforce it with specific feedback, so skills compound fast and confidence stays high.

I "cast" the team by strengths

I tailor ownership, coaching, and rituals to each designer's style — and pair people so strengths complement each other.

I set a high bar and make it achievable

We define quality criteria up front and iterate with focused critique — aiming for excellence, not "good enough."

I build trust first, then speed

I invest in honest, low-ego collaboration — it reduces friction and makes hard product calls easier.

Design processes 🔧

I build processes that increase throughput

Clear intake, prioritization, and definition of done — so designers spend time on impact, not chaos.

I run a clean end-to-end loop

Discovery → concept → validation → delivery → measurement, with explicit handoffs and checklists that work with agile teams.

I standardize what should be standard

Design system usage, patterns, component libraries, and QA routines — so we ship consistent UI and reduce rework.

I balance speed and risk with options

I push for fast prototypes and experiments when uncertainty is high, and decisive execution when it's low.
