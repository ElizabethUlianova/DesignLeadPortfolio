Megamarket

Product Design Lead /
Principal Product Designer

Rethinking a marketplace from scratch and bringing it to life with a 12-person design team.

Main tasks ✔

Rethinking a marketplace

Full redesign of the main marketplace product — improving information architecture, navigation, and the end-to-end shopping experience.

Implement a redesign

Managing a smooth rollout with the development team, monitoring quality at every stage with focused feedback and clear handoff rituals.

Make a strong design force

Building a 12-person design team and design culture around shared standards, clear goals, and continuous professional growth.

Key results 🎁

Measurable design outcomes

Conversion rate to purchase increased 4% to target category. Total GMV of Megamarket increased 8% to 15% per year.

Reduced design lifecycle

The design-to-build time reduced from 8 to 3 weeks. Improved tooling, better prioritisation, and clear design quality bar.

Good quality bar

Launched a uniform design system across the platform, reducing inconsistencies and faster iteration across all squads.

As a Product Design Lead 👥

Leadership challenge

The team was new, the product was complex, and the timeline was tight. I started by defining clear roles and areas of ownership so every designer knew their scope and could make decisions independently.

Working with stakeholders

Regular design reviews and structured sign-off processes — making sure product managers, engineers, and business stakeholders were aligned before we went deep on solutions.

Working with designers

Weekly 1:1s focused on growth, not just status. I introduced structured critique sessions to raise quality standards without slowing down output.

Design rituals

We introduced weekly design showcases, an async design review process, and a "definition of ready" checklist to catch issues early — before engineering picked up the work.

Stakeholder satisfaction

Shifted perception of design from "pixel pushers" to strategic partners — designers were involved earlier in discovery and had a visible impact on product direction.

Designers development

Grew 3 junior designers into mid-level in 12 months. Built an individual development plan framework used across the team.

As an Individual Contributor

In parallel to leading the team, I personally owned the redesign of the main catalogue and search experience — working directly with the data team on discovery, running user testing, and shipping iterative improvements.

I designed the new product card, filter system, and personalised recommendations — coordinating closely with two engineering squads and the data science team to make the experience feel fast and relevant.
